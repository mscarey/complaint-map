DAMAGES TO THE DNC
21. The illegal conspiracy inflicted profound damage upon the DNC. Defendants
undermined and distorted the DNC's ability to communicate the party's values and vision to the
American electorate; sowed discord within the Democratic party at a time when party unity was
essential to electoral success, and seriously compromised the DNC's internal and external
communications.
22.

the DNC. The dissemination of hacked information heightened donors' concerns that confidential
information disclosed through their contributions might be publicly disseminated.
